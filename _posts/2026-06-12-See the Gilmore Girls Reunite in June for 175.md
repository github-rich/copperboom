The ATX Television Festival announced today that as part of their annual celebration of television, they will be reuniting stars Lauren Graham (Lorelai Gilmore), Alexis Bledel (Rory Gilmore) and show creator, writer and director Amy Sherman-Palladino in June next year. 

Expect the air to turn blue.

The festival runs from June 4 to June 7 and you can currently buy a weekend pass for US$175. The $125 passes have already sold out and will go up to $225.
